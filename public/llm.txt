# Problem Statement
problem:
  - Traders and investors rely on signals and charts without quantifying downside risk
  - Most tools show single-point predictions, not probability distributions
  - Users lack visibility into uncertainty, tail risk, and scenario regimes
  - Black-box prediction models don't explain their reasoning
  - No pre-trade risk layer exists for retail and institutional workflows

# Solution Approach
solution:
  - Uses AI to generate probability-weighted scenario regimes
  - Combines quantitative data (prices, volatility, correlations)
  - Incorporates qualitative inputs (sentiment, macro context, uncertainty)
  - Outputs probabilities, downside exposure, and expected ranges
  - Runs 10,000 Monte Carlo simulations per analysis in under 2 seconds
  - Provides AI-generated risk interpretations in clear language
