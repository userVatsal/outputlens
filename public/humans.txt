/* ABOUT */
OutputLens is an AI-powered risk & scenario intelligence platform that uses 
Monte Carlo simulation to calculate Value at Risk (VaR), Expected Shortfall, 
tail risk, and scenario regimes for stocks, crypto, and forex portfolios.

We help traders and institutional investors quantify downside risk before 
making decisions by running 10,000 simulations per analysis in under 2 seconds.

/* CAPABILITIES */
- Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 probabilistic paths)
- Value at Risk (VaR) at 95% and 99% confidence
- Expected Shortfall / Conditional VaR
- Tail risk analysis with extreme scenario modeling
- AI-powered risk interpretations
- Multi-market support (US, UK, EU equities, crypto, forex)
- Portfolio-level correlation and risk insights
- Live market data integration
- Asset tracking and ongoing risk monitoring
