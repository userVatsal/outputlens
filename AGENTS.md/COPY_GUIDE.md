# OutputLens Copywriting Guide

## Tone

Professional.

Analytical.

Institutional.

Educational.

Calm.

---

## Preferred Terms

- Distribution
- Probability
- Risk
- Exposure
- Volatility
- Regime
- Tail Risk
- Expected Shortfall
- Scenario

---

## Avoid

- Guaranteed
- Winning Trade
- Alpha Signal
- Hot Stock
- Moonshot
- Easy Money
- Sure Thing
- Buy Now
- Sell Now

---

## Headlines

Good:

"Risk Conditions Deteriorating"

"Tail Risk Elevated"

"Volatility Regime Shift Detected"

Bad:

"Massive Opportunity Ahead"

"This Stock Could Explode"

"Top Winning Trade"

---

## Commentary

Always describe uncertainty.

Never describe certainty.

Always explain probability ranges.

Never imply future outcomes are known.
