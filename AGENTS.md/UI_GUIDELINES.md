# OutputLens UI Guidelines

## Design Philosophy

OutputLens is an institutional risk platform.

The UI should feel closer to Bloomberg, FactSet, Aladdin, and professional risk terminals than consumer investing apps.

Users come to understand risk, not seek excitement.

---

## Core Principles

1. Risk before return
2. Information before aesthetics
3. Clarity before creativity
4. Data before decoration
5. Analysis before marketing

---

## Visual Style

Preferred:

- Dark theme first
- Dense information layouts
- Professional typography
- Structured dashboards
- High signal-to-noise ratio

Avoid:

- Excessive whitespace
- Marketing-style landing pages
- Oversized illustrations
- Animated backgrounds
- Excessive gradients
- Gamification

---

## Dashboard Rules

Dashboard must always prioritize:

1. Risk Score
2. VaR
3. Tail Risk
4. Regime
5. Alerts
6. Exposure

Never prioritize:

- Potential gains
- Asset popularity
- Social metrics

---

## Charts

Preferred:

- Distribution charts
- Risk curves
- Fan charts
- Correlation matrices
- Drawdown charts

Avoid:

- Decorative charts
- Unexplained visualizations

---

## Colors

Red:
Risk

Amber:
Warning

Green:
Improvement

Never imply certainty through color.

Green does not mean guaranteed gains.

Red does not mean guaranteed losses.

---

## Typography

Professional.

No hype language.

No emotional language.

No marketing superlatives.
