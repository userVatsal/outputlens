# OutputLens MCP Registry

## Market Data MCP

Authority:

Live market data.

Use For:

- Quotes
- Historical Prices
- Volatility Inputs

Never Use For:

- Risk calculations

---

## News MCP

Authority:

Market news and sentiment.

Use For:

- Morning Briefing
- Risk Pulse

Never Use For:

- Investment recommendations

---

## AI Commentary MCP

Authority:

Explanation layer.

Use For:

- Risk interpretation
- Commentary generation

Never Use For:

- Predictions
- Trade recommendations

---

## Principle

Each MCP owns a specific responsibility.

Do not overlap responsibilities.

Respect source authority.
