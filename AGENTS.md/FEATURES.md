# FEATURES.md

# OutputLens Feature Definitions

## Dashboard

Purpose:
Provide portfolio-level awareness.

Primary Metrics:

- Portfolio Risk Score
- Portfolio VaR
- Drawdown
- Regime Status
- Alerts

Question Answered:

"What needs attention today?"

---

## Analysis Workspace

Purpose:
Generate a complete risk profile.

Inputs:

- Asset
- Direction
- Position Size
- Time Horizon

Outputs:

- Distribution
- Win Probability
- VaR
- Expected Shortfall
- Risk Score
- Regime
- Scenarios

This is the primary feature of the platform.

---

## Analysis History

Purpose:

Track historical analyses.

Capabilities:

- Historical review
- Comparison
- Risk evolution tracking

---

## Portfolio Analysis

Purpose:

Aggregate risk across positions.

Focus:

- Correlation
- Concentration
- Exposure
- Portfolio VaR

---

## Morning Briefing

Purpose:

Summarise meaningful changes.

Includes:

- Risk changes
- Regime changes
- Portfolio observations

Does not include:

- Generic market news
- Price predictions

---

## Risk Pulse

Purpose:

Identify changing risk conditions.

Focus:

- Risk score movement
- Regime transitions
- Tail risk changes

---

## Regime Monitor

Purpose:

Monitor market-state changes.

Outputs:

- Current regime
- Regime transitions
- Volatility state

---

## Risk Alerts

Purpose:

Notify users when thresholds are exceeded.

Examples:

- VaR increase
- Regime shift
- Correlation spike

---

## Scenario Builder

Purpose:

Create custom stress scenarios.

Scenarios are:

- Hypothetical
- Exploratory
- Non-predictive

---

## Stress Testing

Purpose:

Evaluate performance under adverse conditions.

Supports:

- Bull
- Base
- Bear
- Custom shocks

---

## VaR Calculator

Purpose:

Standalone risk calculation tool.

Methods:

- Parametric
- Historical
- Monte Carlo

---

## Correlation Scanner

Purpose:

Identify hidden portfolio concentration.

Focus:

- Asset relationships
- Diversification quality

---

## Market Heatmap

Purpose:

Provide market context.

Not:

- Signal generation
- Forecasting

---

## Position Sizer

Purpose:

Support risk-aware allocation decisions.

Not:

- Financial advice
- Trade recommendations

---

## Earnings Risk

Purpose:

Evaluate earnings-event uncertainty.

Focus:

- Historical earnings volatility
- Event risk awareness

---

## Drawdown Analysis

Purpose:

Understand downside history.

Focus:

- Recovery periods
- Historical drawdowns

---

## Volatility Analysis

Purpose:

Understand volatility behaviour.

Focus:

- Historical volatility
- Relative volatility
- Regime volatility
